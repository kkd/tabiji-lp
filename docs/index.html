Vision

Tabiji (tabiji is Japanese, '旅'(tabi) means jouney, '路'(ji) means 'the way') is a journey recording app aiming to make traveling more enjoyable, focusing on traveling. Because I want to make my travel more valuable, I will not dare to use SNS like applications. I do not fully automate because I would like to have my journey memorized more. However, Tabiji is responsible for troublesome things. Why do not you leave a record for yourself after returning home from the extra time? It is not a trip to see just the smartphone, but I want you to look at the actual travel destination and feel it.
We plan and develop such a thing in hope.

Feature

Generated Timeline from photo

Just shoot a picture to make a timeline for the trip! Of course you can write comments!

Multi-views of travel

Look back on the trip from various perspectives such as timeline, map, tags, etc.!

Collection

You can record various memories of travel as collection tag, so you find it quickly!

Offline editing

Offline editing is possible even in places where wifi do not reach, it automatically updates when you arrive where wifi reach!

Timeline View
You can look back on your journey from the time axis, collection tag, map, calendar etc.
Calendar View
The calendar allows you to list the locations and duration of the trip, the events there and so on.
Map View
You can see a combination of traveling maps and events there. At that time, there you can see what happened at a glance.
Collection　View
Souvenirs, meals, things you want to check later, you can attach any collection tags and list them. You can easily find memories of your trip.
Quick Access View
You can quickly retrieve the necessary information at the travel destination, such as itinerary, passport, lodging contact information.
